FAQ

Answers to common questions about Drawing Game and Drawing Game.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes. Dice rolls work with taps, and the board scales responsively for smooth play on phones.

How are game results saved?

Wins, moves, and leaderboard entries are stored directly in your browser using localStorage.

Can I reset the leaderboard?

Yes—open your browser console and run localStorage.removeItem('sl_leaderboard').

What controls are supported?

Click or tap the dice to roll. Use R to restart the match, and P to pause/resume play.

Can I customize the look?

Absolutely—adjust CSS variables in assets/css/style.css and tweak HTML text for your own theme.
